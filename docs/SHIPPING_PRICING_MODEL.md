## How to explain this simply:
*   CircuCity charges shipping at PostNord cost.
*   A **10% handling fee** is added to cover logistics coordination and platform operations.
*   CircuCity does not take commission from the item price.

### Example:
If PostNord shipping costs **143.20 SEK**, CircuCity adds **14.32 SEK**. The buyer pays **157.52 SEK**, and CircuCity earns **14.32 SEK** on shipping.
